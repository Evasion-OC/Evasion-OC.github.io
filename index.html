About Experience Research Posts

I work at the intersection of graph theory, algorithms, and discrete mathematics, with applied interests in machine learning and recommendation systems. My broader interests sit between theory and practical engineering — I'm drawn to problems where a careful argument or experiment is worth a thousand opinions.

I earned my MSc in Computer Science from the University of Greenwich and my BSc in Mathematics from the University of Leeds.

Research interests

  • Graph theory — including structural graph theory and graph algorithms.
  • Network science — modelling and analysing complex networks.
  • Combinatorics, discrete mathematics & algorithms — the foundations.
  • Machine learning — deep learning and recommendation systems.
